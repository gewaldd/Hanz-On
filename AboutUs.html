about us

At Hanz-On Airsoft Supplies, we’re more than just an online airsoft retail store. We're a community built by fans for fans. Founded with a passion for the sport, our mission is to provide players of all levels with reliable gear, high-quality equipment, and the right accessories to elevate every game. Hanz-On Airsoft Supplies is your reliable partner in every fight, with a promise of great customer service, quick shipment, and real support. From tactical gear and protective equipment to replicas and upgrades, we’ve got you covered—so you can focus on what truly matters.

WHO WE ARE

WE ARE 3RD YEAR INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY STUDENTS OF THE TECHNOLOGICAL INSTITUTE OF THE PHILIPPINES. We love playing airsoft so much that we opened a store for the community. We know the excitement of gearing up, the importance of reliable equipment, and the value of having the right tools in the field. That’s why we built a store that offers quality products, fair prices, and dependable service for players of all skill levels.
